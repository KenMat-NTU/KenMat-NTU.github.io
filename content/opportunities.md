        <div id="pd-opportunities">We currently do <b>not</b> have open positions for postdoctoral positions but highly encourage you to apply for <b>scholarship/fellowship programmes</b>. Please <b>contact Ken first</b> if you are interested in any postdoctoral experience at MoSPSyDe.</div>

        ---
        
        ### Available Scholarships

        **1. [Lee Kuan Yew Postdoctoral Fellowship (LKYPDF)](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/research/research-careers/lee-kuan-yew-postdoctoral-fellowship-(lkypdf))**  
        - **Eligibility:** Recent PhD graduates, or with at most 1 postdoctoral training from a reputable university  
        - **Timeline:** Typically Nov–Feb (Currently closed)

        **2. [Presidential Postdoctoral Fellowship (PPF)](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/research/research-careers/presidential-postdoctoral-fellowship-(ppf))**  
        - **Eligibility:** Within 5 years of obtaining PhD or equivalent degree  
        - **Timeline:** TBA

        **3. [The Eric and Wendy Schmidt AI in Science Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/research/research-careers/the-eric-and-wendy-schmidt-ai-in-science-postdoctoral-fellowship)**   
        - **Eligibility:** Within 3 years of having obtained PhD degree or equivalent degree in non-computer science areas  
        - **Timeline:** Typically December

        **4. [NTU AI-for-X Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/research/research-careers/ntu-ai-for-x-postdoctoral-fellowship)**   
        - **Eligibility:** PhD conferred within the past 3 years in a STEM field  
        - **Timeline:** Typically October

        **5. [SG Academies South-East Asia Fellowship](https://snas.org.sg/aboutsaseaf)**   
        - **Eligibility:** ASEAN citizen (non-Singapore citizen or PR) with PhD  
        - **Timeline:** Typically August

        **6. External fellowship from your country**   
        - Many countries support their PhD graduates to pursue their postdoc overseas, including NTU. We strongly recommend to check these opportunities first.  
        - **Examples:**
          - From EU: [Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/actions/postdoctoral-fellowships)
          - From Japan: [JSPS Overseas Research Fellowships](https://www.jsps.go.jp/english/e-ab/)

      title: 'PhD Student Positions'
      text: |
        <div id="phd-opportunities">NTU has two intakes annualy: <b>August</b> and <b>January</b>. The deadline is usually five months prior to the admission.</div>

        - All students need to fulfil the [admission requirements](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/cceb/admissions/detail/scbe-doctor-of-philosophy-(phd)-(by-research)#programme) at CCEB.
        - All applicants (except for self-funded students) are strongly encouraged to apply for NTU scholarships (you need to declare in the application).
        - Prospective students may consider external scholarship opportunities or self-funding since funded positions are very limited.
          - [A*STAR Graduate Scholarship](https://www.a-star.edu.sg/scholarships/home/scholarships/ags--scholarship): carefully read the website since eligibility is limited.
          - External graduate scholarship from your home countries or industrial funding
          - Please check [here](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/admissions/graduate/financialmatters/pgtuitionfees) to consider a self-funding option.
        - Please <b>contact Ken in advance</b> if you want to apply.
